Arrabbiata

Home

Description

Arrabbiata is an easy, weeknight dinner when you don't feel like cooking after work. A perfect blend of tomatoey sweetness, garlic goodness, and a hint of spice. It's sure to be a hit with anyone!

Ingredients

  • Dry pasta of your choice (penne is recommended) - 300g
  • Canned tomatoes - 1 can
  • Tomato paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Brown onion - 1
  • Garlic - 4 cloves
  • Red chilli - To your liking
  • Olive oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt and pepper - To your liking
  • Basil - To your liking
  • Vegan cheese - For topping

Steps

  1. Dice onion and finely mince garlic. If using chilli, finely chop it now.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large fry pan on medium heat. Once hot, add onion.
  3. Once onion becomes soft and translucent, add garlic and chilli. Add in the tomato paste, and cook until the paste darkens and the garlic is fragrant.
  4. Add in the canned tomatoes, and reduce to low heat. Keep on low heat until the pasta is ready.
  5. After adding the tomatoes, bring a pot of salted water to the boil.
  6. Once boiled, add pasta and cook as per instructions.
  7. Once cooked, drain the pasta, reserving a small amount of pasta water (100mL).
  8. Add the pasta to the fry pan, with a little of the reserved pasta water.
  9. Mix well, and cook until the pasta water has evaporated.
  10. Serve immediately, topping with basil and vegan cheese.
